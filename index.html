Stadiums

The 64 matches of the FIFA World Cup 2022 tournament were organized in 8 magnificent football stadiums across Qatar

Lusail Stadium

Capacity: 88,966

The crown jewel of Qatar 2022, hosting the opening ceremony and final match.

Khalifa Stadium

Capacity: 45,857

Qatar's oldest stadium, beautifully renovated for the tournament.

Al Janoub Stadium

Capacity: 44,325

Inspired by traditional dhow boats, designed by Zaha Hadid.

Education City Stadium

Capacity: 44,667

Known as the "Diamond of the Desert" for its stunning facade.

Ahmad bin Ali Stadium

Capacity: 45,032

Built using materials from the demolished old stadium.

Al Bayt Stadium

Capacity: 68,895

Designed to resemble a traditional Bedouin tent.

Al Thumama Stadium

Capacity: 44,400

Design inspired by the gahfiya, a traditional woven cap.

Stadium 974

Capacity: 44,089

Unique stadium built entirely from shipping containers.
